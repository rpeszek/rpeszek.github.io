Infrequent: I do not blog much. Besides, there are many excellent functional programming blogs already. I will commit a post if something really bugs me or I feel very passionate about a topic that is not otherwise covered by people smarted than me.

Pragmatic: My pet peeve is identifying patterns of erroneous code and what could be causing them, there are technical and human factors underlying these patterns. I particular, I am trying to understand how certain code abstractions could promote erroneous code. The term anti-pattern has been used for something similar. However, that term is associated with design patterns and this topic is broader.
